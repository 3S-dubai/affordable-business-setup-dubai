Common Questions

Pricing Questions Answered

What does AED 4,999 actually include?

The starting price covers your trade license, government registration, establishment card, and PRO services. Visa fees and office space are optional extras quoted separately and shown upfront.

Are there any hidden fees I should know about?

No. We give you a full cost breakdown before you commit. The price we quote is the price you pay. Any optional extras like visas or office space are listed separately - never added in surprise.

Why is the freezone option cheaper than mainland?

Freezone companies require fewer government approvals and have a simpler registration process. Mainland involves additional DET requirements and is suited for businesses that need direct access to UAE local markets.

Can I upgrade my setup later if my business grows?

Yes. You can add visa quotas, upgrade your office space, add business activities, or even migrate from freezone to mainland later. Our team advises on the most cost-effective path as you grow.

Is a cheap setup still a quality setup?

Absolutely. Lower cost does not mean lower quality. Our pricing reflects efficient processes, direct jurisdiction partnerships, and volume - not shortcuts. Every setup is fully compliant and properly registered.

What if I am not sure which setup suits me?

That is exactly what our free consultation is for. Tell us your business activity and goals - we recommend the most suitable and cost-effective option with no pressure to proceed.

Why is your price so much lower than everyone else?

Two reasons. First, we work directly with freezone authorities as registered associates - no middlemen adding their margin. Second, our volume allows us to operate on tighter margins without cutting corners. The license you receive is identical to what an AED 15,000 package elsewhere would produce. The difference is cost structure, not quality.

What happens after I pay - how do I know the process moves forward?

You get a dedicated consultant assigned on day one. They share a step-by-step timeline over WhatsApp so you can see exactly where things stand at all times. Most freezone licenses are issued within 5 working days. If anything needs more time, your consultant tells you directly - you are never left waiting without an update.

Can I open a UAE bank account with this freezone license?

Yes. A properly issued freezone trade license and establishment card are the documents banks require. We guide you through the bank account process after your license is ready - including which banks work best for your business type and nationality. This is part of our post-setup guidance at no extra charge.

What is the renewal cost after the first year?

Renewal is charged by the freezone authority directly and is separate from our service fee. For Meydan, renewal typically falls in a similar range to the first year. We show you the expected renewal cost upfront before you commit - so there are no surprises at the 12-month mark.
